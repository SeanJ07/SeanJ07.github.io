⟨ about ⟩

The short version

I'm Sean Jenkins. I'm a CSUSB student who builds things and helps other people build things too.

In high school I founded Hourhand Studios and served as Creative Director and Social Media Manager. We took 4th at the SkillsUSA California State Championship with our game Beneath Arcantum — 53 C# scripts, 12 scenes, finished on a deadline that didn't move.

Now I run social media for CSUSB Graduate Studies, build AI tools on the side, and work toward Jenkins Digital Design — a STEAM education platform where each of us teaches what we know. Music, cybersecurity, astronomy, code. The best way to learn something is to teach it.

When I'm not working, I'm watching Gurren Lagann, building in Minecraft, or convincing someone they can do engineering even if they don't think they can.
