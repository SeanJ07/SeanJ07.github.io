The short version

I'm Sean Jenkins — CS student at CSUSB (B.S. Computer Systems, 3.42 GPA, expected 2029). I specialize in game development and software engineering.

In high school I co-founded Hourhand Studios and ran it as Creative Director and Social Media Manager. We shipped Beneath Arcantum, a steampunk puzzle-platformer, and took 4th place at the SkillsUSA California State Championship. I handled everything from brand identity to social media to production docs.

Before that I spent two years as a Teaching Assistant helping high schoolers learn C# and Unity — which taught me how to explain technical concepts without doing the work for people. I hold Unity certifications in VR Development, Programming, and Art.

Right now I'm building Spool (a Python music player), automation tools, and web UIs. Looking for roles in game development, software engineering, or digital media where I can contribute immediately.
