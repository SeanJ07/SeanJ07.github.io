⟨ about ⟩

The short version

I’m Sean Jenkins, a CSUSB student who builds things and helps other people build things too.

In high school I founded Hourhand Studios and led a team to 4th place at the SkillsUSA California State Championship with our game Beneath Arcantum. That taught me what it means to ship under pressure. 53 C# scripts and 12 scenes, finished on a deadline that didn’t move.

Now I’m building AI tools and content systems, and working toward Jenkins Digital Design, a STEAM education platform where each of us teaches what we’re passionate about. Music production. Cybersecurity. Astronomy. Code. Because the best way to learn something is to teach it.

When I’m not coding, I’m watching Gurren Lagann, building in Minecraft, or encouraging someone into engineering who didn’t think they could do it.
