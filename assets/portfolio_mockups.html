PostCard QR Code — Strategy Context
Original postcard design featuring an integrated QR code, demonstrating real-world marketing application — connecting print materials to digital content.

PostCard QR Code — Design Strategy

Objective: Create a physical print piece that bridges offline and online engagement by integrating a QR code directly into the design composition.

This postcard was designed to serve as both a standalone visual piece and a functional marketing tool. The QR code is not an afterthought — it was incorporated into the layout from the start, positioned to draw the eye naturally from the headline to the call-to-action. When scanned, it links directly to an event registration page, converting physical impressions into measurable digital engagement.

Skills demonstrated: Integrated print/digital marketing strategy · Adobe Photoshop layout design · QR code implementation · Visual hierarchy · Call-to-action placement

Sticker Series — Engagement Strategy

Objective: Design a set of original digital stickers that could be used across Instagram stories, posts, and messaging — encouraging shares and brand awareness.

The 8-sticker series demonstrates an understanding of how shareable visual content drives engagement on social platforms. Each sticker was individually designed in Adobe Photoshop with consistent style, ensuring brand cohesion across the set. The physical print version (PhysicalStickers.png) shows how digital designs translate to tangible products for events and promotions.

Skills demonstrated: Digital illustration · Brand consistency · Social media engagement strategy · Print-to-digital production pipeline · Adobe Photoshop
